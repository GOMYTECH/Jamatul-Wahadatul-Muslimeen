Program Highlights

Our youth initiatives combine Islamic education with personal development in an engaging format:

Annual Youth Retreat

3-day residential program featuring Islamic workshops, outdoor activities, and leadership training (dates announced annually).

Online Classes

Weekly virtual sessions on Quran study, contemporary Islamic issues, and career guidance for young Muslims.

Brotherhood Circles

Monthly meetups grouped by age brackets (15-20, 21-25, 26-35) for tailored Islamic discussions and networking.

Community Service

Quarterly projects where youth organize charity drives, mosque cleanups, and Islamic outreach programs.

Tahfiz Program

Structured Quran memorization tracks with qualified teachers and progress certifications.

Career Guidance

Workshops on Islamically-responsible career paths, CV building, and entrepreneurship from Muslim professionals.
