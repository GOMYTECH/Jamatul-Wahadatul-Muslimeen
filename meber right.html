Membership Rights & Obligations

Understand your privileges and responsibilities as a member of Jamatul Wahadatul Muslimeen

Member Rights

As a registered member of JWM, you are entitled to the following privileges:

Spiritual Guidance

Access to qualified Islamic scholars and regular tafseer sessions for personal and family spiritual development.

Educational Programs

Free or subsidized access to all educational programs including Quran classes, Arabic courses, and Islamic studies.

Social Support

Eligibility for zakat and sadaqah funds when in need, marriage counseling, and family support services.

Voting Rights

Right to vote in organizational elections after 1 year of active membership and participation.

Member Obligations

All members are required to fulfill these responsibilities to maintain active membership status:

Program Attendance

Mandatory attendance at key programs including Sunday lectures, yearly seminars, and Ramadan activities.

Youth Participation

Members aged 15-35 are automatically enrolled in Tahfiz Youth Group and must participate in at least 70% of activities.

Financial Commitment

Timely payment of all obligatory donations including monthly sadaqah and special program fees as determined by the Amir.

Event Participation

Active involvement in at least 3 organizational events annually, with assigned duties to be completed satisfactorily.

Mandatory Programs Attendance

All members must attend these core programs annually (minimum 80% attendance):

Weekly Sunday Program

Every Sunday 9am-12pm - Islamic lectures and group discussions

Annual 3-Day Seminar

Held every November - Intensive Islamic training and workshops

Ramadan Programs

Daily tafsir sessions and weekend iftar gatherings during Ramadan

Quarterly Members Meeting

Organizational updates and planning sessions

Financial hardship exemptions available upon written request to the Amir.

Membership Agreement

By maintaining membership with Jamatul Wahadatul Muslimeen, I agree to:

  • Fulfill all religious and organizational obligations
  • Maintain good conduct representing Islamic values
  • Attend required programs regularly
  • Meet all financial commitments promptly
  • Follow the guidance of the Amir in organizational matters

Membership may be reviewed annually based on fulfillment of these obligations.
