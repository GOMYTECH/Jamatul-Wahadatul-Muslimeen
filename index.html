About Our Organization

Jamatul Wahadatul Muslimeen is a Nigeria-based Islamic organization established in 1995 with the mission to promote unity among Muslims, provide authentic Islamic education, and serve the community according to the teachings of the Quran and Sunnah.

With branches in 12 states across Nigeria, we have impacted thousands of lives through our educational programs, dawah activities, and community development initiatives.
